About Us

Hatfield Jame Masjid (Mosque) is a tax-exempt 501(C) (3) religious organization, located in Hatfield, Pennsylvania. This organization was established on June 2007 to practice religious beliefs for the local Muslim community. It is a rapidly growing worship center in our Muslim community. In addition to offering services for five-time Salah a day, we are proud of organizing Saturday and Sunday teaching classes for our youths to learn Quran and Hadith. We also run a Quran learning class for adults five days in a week, and Hadith discussing sessions every day after Isha prayer. We also host activities such as arranging scholarly talks on important topics addressing the community-related issues or challenges based on Quran and Hadith for weekend students and for all. To arrange educational competition, including Quran recitation, Islamic essay competition have proven the best way for our youth to learn faster about our beautiful religion. Please visit us at your convenience.
